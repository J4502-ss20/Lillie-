The "GO" Series

This series finds its basis from my favorite movie quote. In the movie, Stuck in Love, one of the characters - a writer - tells his developing writer son:

"A writer is the sum of their experiences. Go get some."

So, essentially that is what I am trying to do: just a bunch of different things! Here, I will write and reflect on a wide variety of things.

AND...there is not really a limit to these things. That would seem to defeat the entire purpose.

The Macaroni and Cheese Series

Similar to the Copycat Series, this series is a sort of experiment. In the last 4 or so months of 2020, a lot of things have happened:

(1) I have purchased a beautiful dress for my July 2020 wedding.

(2) After learning about a disease I have called hashimoto's disease, I was told by my doctor to remove gluten from my diet.

(3) I have gained some unwanted weight.

So... in order to make sure that dress fits just right on my wedding day, I need to work hard to adopt a healthy, gluten-free diet.

But there is another issue: I barely know how to cook!

My previous attempts to rely on my own cooking even resulted in lots of Macaroni and Cheese, pizza, cerea and peanut-butter and jelly sandwiches, all of which contain - you guessed it - gluten!.

So...here's my attempt to teach myself how to cook healthy food without that magical gluten ingredient. We'll see how it goes!

The Copycat Series

In this series, I take on the challenge of writing with a wide variety of styles, voices and technuqies.

First, I will choose a book to read. Then after reading it, I will attempt to write a short story or written piece of any sort that matches the style, voice, and writing techniques of the story I just read.

And I will post these attempts here for you all to see. Feel free to send me your thoughts. I'd welcom that!

Of course, I will not by copying the plotline of these books. I will just be analyzing the authors' techniques and trying them out on my own.

Through this series, I hope to learn how to write more creatively in a wide variety of ways and determine which style best suits me.
