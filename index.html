Welcome to Margaret's Muse!

My name is Lillie Margaret Hegeman.

I am a data journalism student just finishing my third year at the University of Missouri - Columbia.

I created this website from scratch during the 2020 Spring semester in a Multimedia Planning and Design class through the J-School. As I continue through my schooling and into the field as a journalist or communicator, I hope to use this website not only to showcase the content I have created and published with various organizations, but also to explore my own creativity and voice in my personal blog.
